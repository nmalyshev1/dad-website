Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you request a quote or contact us, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name and contact details (email address, phone number).
  • Trip details (pickup/drop-off locations, dates, passenger count, luggage).
  • Special requirements you choose to share with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide our premium chauffeur services, which includes:

  • Processing quotes and coordinating transfers.
  • Communicating with you regarding your booking.
  • Improving our website and services.

3. Data Protection (GDPR)

As a French company, V-TRANSFER complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data is only shared with our employed chauffeurs and logistics personnel strictly and to the extent necessary to fulfill your booked services.
