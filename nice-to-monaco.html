The Smoothest Way from Nice Airport to Monaco

Landing at Nice Côte d'Azur Airport (NCE) and heading to Monaco or Monte-Carlo? Forget the slow public bus or the uncertainty of a standard taxi rank. V-Transfer provides a seamless, fixed-price private chauffeur experience that gets you from arrivals to the heart of Monaco in approximately 30 minutes.

Your professional driver will be waiting at the arrivals hall with a personalised name board, ready to assist with your luggage and escort you to a pristine Mercedes-Benz E-Class or S-Class. Every vehicle in our fleet is meticulously maintained, offering premium leather interiors, climate control, and a serene, quiet ride — perfect for decompressing after a long flight or preparing for an important meeting in the Principality.

Why Choose a Private Transfer Instead of Alternatives?

  • Fixed price — no hidden costs, no surge pricing, agreed before you book
  • Real-time flight tracking — your driver adjusts automatically for delays
  • Meet & Greet included — we come inside the terminal, straight to arrivals
  • Discreet & private — no shared rides, no strangers
  • Mercedes-Benz E-Class or V-Class — executive-standard comfort all the way
  • 24/7 availability — early morning and late-night arrivals catered for

About This Route

The route from Nice airport to Monaco follows the scenic coastal road along the French Riviera, passing through Villefranche-sur-Mer and Èze. Your chauffeur will select the optimal route based on traffic conditions to ensure the most comfortable and timely journey possible.

V-Transfer is based in Théoule-sur-Mer and serves the entire Côte d'Azur — from Nice and Cannes to Antibes, Monaco, Cap Ferrat, and Saint-Tropez. Whether you need a one-way airport run or a full-day chauffeur at your disposal, we are here.
